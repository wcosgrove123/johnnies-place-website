## An Epic Journey for a Great Cause

In honor of America's 250th anniversary, two dedicated riders are embarking on a coast-to-coast bicycle journey from Ocean Shores, Washington to Avalon, New Jersey. This incredible 3,664-mile adventure will take place over 46 days, from May 20 to July 4, 2026.

## The Mission

Every mile pedaled, every hill climbed, and every state crossed represents our commitment to creating safe, supportive housing for adults with autism. This fundraiser directly supports Johnnie's Place in building a sustainable model for community care.

## The Route

- **Distance**: 3,664 miles
- **Duration**: 46 days
- **Start**: Ocean Shores, Washington (May 20, 2026)
- **Finish**: Avalon, New Jersey (July 4, 2026)
- **States**: Crossing 14 states from sea to shining sea

## How Your Support Helps

Your donation to this fundraiser goes directly toward:

- Residential housing improvements and adaptations
- Community programming and activities
- Support services and professional staffing
- Building a replicable model for other communities

Track our riders' progress, read daily updates, and follow this incredible journey on the [Sea to Shining Sea website](../sea-to-shining-sea/index.html).

Every contribution, no matter the size, brings us closer to our goal and makes a real difference in the lives of adults with autism.
